About Pokemon

Pokémon is a beloved franchise that began with video games and expanded into TV shows, movies, trading cards, and more. Players explore a world filled with unique creatures called Pokémon, which they catch, train, and battle. Its themes of friendship, adventure, and strategy have captured hearts around the world for decades.
